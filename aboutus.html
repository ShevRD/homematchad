How to do a best match search:

  1. Sign up or login into the website
  2. After you have logged into your account, you'll see buttons on the screen which allows you to navigate through the different parts of the website.
  3. Click on Best Match Search.
  4. For each feature on the form, select yes if you would want that feature or no if you would not want it. Beside that enter the rating(how much you want that feature) on a scale from one to five(1-5).
  5. Submit the form and wait.
  6. The user will be presented with a list showing their best match and 9 other recommended homes.
