About

Homeowners do not have a means of efficiently and optimally matching any and all their available houses to their potential customers who would be interested in the features their house(s) has to offer. Likewise, potential homebuyers would like an easier way to find themselves a house that best suits their needs and wants.

This web application is designed to allow home sellers to enlist their properties and the features they have based on Twelve(12) pre-defined features. Home buyers will then be able to do a criterion based search and also a best match search. The best match search will use the features that the homebuyer wants along with the ratings for those features to provide the best matched house for that user and also other top 9 recommendend houses.

Our features include:
  • Messaging between users
  • General Search for properties
  • Best Match House
  • View submitted properties
